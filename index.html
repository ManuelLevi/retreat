Are you really free?

Much of us live in a prision. No-one put us there, we've built it ourselves.
 This prision is made of bad habits, behaviours and self-limiting beliefs. We've become addicts to dopamine and we lack the self-control to do what we have to. Some of us are work-a-holics while others can't get up to start doing anything meaningful.
Life isn't bad, but it isn't what we want it to be.

Now imagine replacing the prision of yourself with a new type of a controlled environment.
A place where your bad decisions are limited, and everyone wants you to succeed.
A place where everything is built to remove bad habits and build new positive behaviours.
A place where you could build your own self-discipline to finally find true freedom.
A place where you could finally focus on your health.
A place where you don't have to make any decisions, besides the ones that will change your life for the better, forever.
We're building a place for permanent, positive and fast changes in your life.

Manta Retreat

Taking control of your live

Light daily exercise

We all know light exercise is healthy for you. But that doesn't mean you'll do it consistently. During the retreat, there will be some light exercise classes focused on mobility, flexibility, and longevity.

Healthy Diet

We have a healthy meal plan prepared for the whole week, so you don't have to choose what to eat next while trusting it will be tasty and healthy.

Digital Detox

No laptops, no phones, and no smart watches allowed.

Community

You'll be joining a community facing the same challenges. A community of free, independent and positive people who understand health is important and that education and discipline are essential.

Sleep

Most of the developed world is sleep-deprived. We'll have a sleep expert help you build positive patterns for a healthy duration and quality of your sleep. Artificial light will be limited during the evening.

Personalized and Private

You are free to focus on whatever change you desire, and keep it to yourself and no-one else. It's your decision. Your space.
